# Metric Definitions

## Product Sales (before returns)

Business-facing meaning of `net_gmv` in this project.  
It represents positive merchandise sales only.

## Return Burden

Business-facing meaning of `return_rate`.  
It measures the load of merchandise returns relative to merchandise activity.

## Top50 SKU Coverage

Share of monthly product sales contributed by the top 50 SKUs.

## SKU HHI

Overall SKU concentration indicator based on sales-share distribution.

## Identified Sales Coverage

Share of product sales linked to known customer IDs.  
This defines the interpretation boundary of customer-level analysis.

## Next-Month Retention

Share of identified customers from one month who return in the following month.

## Top10 Customer Share

Share of identified-customer sales contributed by the top 10 customers.

## Customer HHI

Overall customer concentration indicator based on identified-customer sales-share distribution.

## Top Return SKUs

Products contributing the most to merchandise return burden.  
Used for **driver discovery only**, not root-cause proof.

## MoM Variance

Supporting signal showing month-on-month KPI movement.  
Not a primary dashboard module.

## Cohort Output

Supporting customer-count output by cohort month and period index.  
Not a primary dashboard module.
